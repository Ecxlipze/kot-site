Our Approach to hybrid workplace flexibility

At KOT Enterprises, flexibility is not just a benefit, it’s a core enabler of productivity and innovation. We embrace a hybrid work culture that empowers our team members to perform at their best wherever and whenever they work most effectively. By supporting diverse workstyles across locations, hours, and environments, we’re creating a work experience that adapts to our people, not the other way around.

The future of
work is here

At KOT Enterprises, whether you’re working from home or the office, we equip you with the tools, technology, and trust you need to perform at your best. Our flexible work culture is rooted in autonomy, accountability, and mutual trust—empowering you to choose how you work while staying aligned with our goals and fostering strong collaboration and innovation.

Working Models

Work site

This is the physical place where you work. Work site information provided in a job posting indicates the expectations of working remotely or working on-site.

Work location

This is the geographic location of your work. Each organization at Microsoft has guidelines related to location flexibility.

Work hours

This encompasses the hours and days when you work. We encourage employees to work the hours that are best for them and that allow them to deliver according to expectations.

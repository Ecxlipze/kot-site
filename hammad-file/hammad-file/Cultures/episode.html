KOT's Early in Profession experience

At KOT Enterprises, our Early in Profession experience is a launchpad for innovators, learners, and future leaders. Whether you're fresh out of school, self-taught, a bootcamp graduate, or making a career shift we offer you the chance to explore new horizons in technology and leadership. We believe in placing individuals where their skills thrive, passions grow, and ideas inspire. This isn’t just the start of a job it’s the beginning of a purpose-driven journey, where you’re empowered to make a meaningful impact. Step into your future with KOT and shape the world with us.

Internship programs

Global university internships

Intern at KOT Enterprises and work on real projects, innovate, and grow open to students from all programs.

Discovery program

Our four-week Discovery Program offers passionate, tech-driven students the chance to explore real projects, gain mentorship, and discover career paths—empowering future changemakers through hands-on experience.

PhD, MBA, JD internships

Pursuing a PhD, MBA, or JD? Join KOT to work on impactful, innovative projects while gaining hands-on experience as a valued part of our team.

Corporate, External, and Legal Affairs (CELA) internships

A unique chance for future legal professionals to gain real-world experience and grow their careers within a leading tech-driven environment.

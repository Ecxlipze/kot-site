Interview tips for all roles 

FIND JOBS

Candidate code of conduct

As you progress through our recruitment process, the next steps may vary depending on the role you’re applying for. Be prepared to demonstrate how your background and experience align with the job requirements by sharing specific examples or ideas on how you would approach certain tasks.

We encourage you to highlight how the skills you’ve developed throughout your career can contribute to the role at KOT. Depending on the position, you may be asked to write code, showcase a creative portfolio, or share work samples in other relevant formats.

You'll have the opportunity to meet with several team members—both from the core team and cross-functional departments—for up to an hour each. This is a chance for us to get to know you better, and for you to learn more about our culture, team dynamics, and the work we do at KOT.

After the interviews, your recruiter will provide clarity on the next steps and timeline for the hiring decision

Please note: Our interviews are currently conducted entirely online. For additional support, feel free to explore our guide on preparing for virtual interviews.

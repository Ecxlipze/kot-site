Candidate code of conduct

At KOT Enterprises, we value candidates who embody our core principles of respect, integrity, and accountability throughout every stage of the hiring journey.

Authentic Representation:

We expect candidates to present themselves truthfully across all phases of the recruitment process including resumes, applications, assessments, interviews, and any pre-onboarding interactions.

Ethical Use of AI:

We support the responsible and ethical use of AI. Candidates are welcome to use AI tools to aid in their preparation, as long as the usage is honest and reflective of their actual capabilities. However, during assessments and interviews, we trust that candidates will demonstrate their skills independently without external help, resources, or AI unless otherwise specified.

Commitment to Responsible AI Principles:

KOT Enterprises is committed to upholding the principles of accountability, fairness, inclusivity, transparency, safety, reliability, and privacy in all AI interactions. We expect candidates to align with these values when engaging with our technologies and processes.
